Coastal Fish Curry

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g firm white fish fillets
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate fish in lemon juice for 15 minutes
  2. Saut√© onions until golden, add garlic and ginger
  3. Add spices and cook until fragrant (1 minute)
  4. Pour in tomato puree and cook for 3 minutes
  5. Add coconut milk and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Gently add fish pieces and cook for 8-10 minutes
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice

Nutritional Notes

Rich in omega-3s | High protein | Gluten-free
