Fragrant Coconut Rice

Prep: 5 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear
  2. Heat coconut oil in pot, add whole spices
  3. Add rice and toast for 2 minutes
  4. Pour in coconut milk and water
  5. Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer
  6. Cover and cook for 15 minutes
  7. Remove from heat, let steam for 5 minutes
  8. Fluff with fork and garnish

Serving Suggestions

Pairs perfectly with curries | Great with grilled fish | Vegan-friendly
